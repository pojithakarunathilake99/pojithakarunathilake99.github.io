About Me

Educator. Engineer. Mentor.

I'm Pojitha Hettiarachchilalage — a tech professional, educator and mentor based in the UK. I hold a Master's in Artificial Intelligence from Birmingham City University and a BSc in Computer Security from NSBM Green University (affiliated with Plymouth University, UK).

I currently work as an IoT Engineer at CompleteCyber, building cutting-edge cybersecurity inspection devices. I also volunteer as an AI Lecturer at Genius Biut Academy, where I teach and inspire the next generation of tech talent.

My passion is making technology education accessible to everyone — from young learners aged 12 taking their first steps in coding, to professionals navigating career transitions in tech.
